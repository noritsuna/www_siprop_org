### Rule
1. touch the light.
    1. The light path and shapes is difficult to change freely, but slime light can be easy to use, as users like.
    1. touch the light/change the light shapes/bend light shapes/mix light/peel off the light
1. touch the heart.
    1. This application have two heartbeat sensor, shine with the detected heartbeat.
    1. slime lamp represent the light of heartbeat.
1. others
    1. The users play by touching the light freely in these two functions.And project the scene of  the using the slime lamp by projector, not only the playing user, audience enjoy watching that scene.
